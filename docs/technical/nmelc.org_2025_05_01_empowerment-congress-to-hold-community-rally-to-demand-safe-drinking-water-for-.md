Thursday, May 1, 2025
Empowerment Congress to Hold Community Rally To Demand Safe Drinking Water for All CRRUA Customers and Consumers
When: Monday, May 5th, 2025, 5PM to 6PM
Where: Sunland Park Sports Complex, 4700 McNutt Rd, Santa Teresa, NM 88008
Why: Community Rally for Clean and Safe Drinking Water
Sunland Park, NM—Community members, local residents, customers of Camino Real Regional Utility Authority (CRRUA) and everyone who cares about environmental justice are invited to a community-led Rally to Demand Safe Drinking Water.
The rally will be held on Monday, May 5th from 5pm to 6pm at the Sunland Park Sports Complex located at 4700 McNutt Road in Santa Teresa, New Mexico.
José Saldaña, Sunland Park resident, said, “Enough of the lying. The truth has to come out. We will not give up. God bless us all.”
Daisy Maldonado, Director of Empowerment Congress, said, “It’s No Corruption Summer in Sunland Park.” …
