Do My Job: Streamline Auto & Cycle Collision
Streamline Auto & Cycle Collision has two locations -- one in Central and the other in East El Paso. Both are owned by Annette Hernandez, a woman who's been in the automotive industry for more than 20 years. Hernandez manages the Central location, while her daughter manages the one in East El Paso. She tells KFOX14 being the boss in a male-dominated industry was tough at first. Hernandez said there were many times when customers did not take her seriously as the manager and owner.
