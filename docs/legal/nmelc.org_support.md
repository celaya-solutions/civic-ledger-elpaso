Get Involved in the Fight for Environmental Justice
I want to support the Law Center’s current work.
Your support to NMELC’s Annual Fund is crucial to standing up for environmental justice for the communities that we serve and ensuring that New Mexico will be a beautiful, healthy place for generations to come.
I want to help sustain the Law Center’s work on an ongoing basis.
Your sustaining gift will automatically repeat each month until you choose to cancel it. Monthly gifts provide vital funds that support the Law Center’s work on an ongoing basis.
I want to support the Law Center’s future.
By providing for the Law Center in your will or other estate plans, through the Meiklejohn Legacy Circle, you will sustain the Law Center’s fight for environmental justice well into the future.
I shop on Amazon.com, I want to support the Law Center’s mission.
Experience feel-good shopping through Amazon Smile. Designate the Law Center as your charitable organization when you shop, at no extra cost to you.
I want to donate a gift of stock.
We are happy to accept gifts of stock or securities. Please click here for more information about gifting publicly traded securities to support the Law Center.
I want to create my own fundraiser.
You can host an online fundraiser at Crowdrise.com to engage your family and friends in supporting the Law Center. Contact Dr. Virginia Necochea for more information.
