MAKE A GIFT: CASH, SECURITIES AND APPRECIATED STOCKS
You may make gifts of publicly traded stocks, cash, bonds and mutual funds. Securities which have an unrealized long term capital gain may be a more effective gift than cash.
DONOR INTENT NOTIFICATION
In order for the New Mexico Environmental Law Center to acknowledge your gift and for audit purposes, please provide us with the following information:
• Donor’s name and address
• The name and number of securities transferred
• Your broker’s contact information
Once we have this information, we will send you our brokers name and contact information, and the account routing number necessary for the transfer.
