Industrial and mining activities that pollute the air steal years away from the people who live nearby.
Those who live in low-income neighborhoods and neighborhoods of color die younger than their counterparts in richer, whiter neighborhoods. The Law Center represents residents in these communities in their battle to fend off polluting industry and preserve their right to clean air.
