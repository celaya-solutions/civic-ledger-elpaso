ENVIRONMENTAL JUSTICE SERIES
Join us for important conversations focused on environmental justice in New Mexico.
Environmental justice is the fair treatment and meaningful involvement of all people regardless of race, color, national origin, or income, with respect to the development, implementation, and enforcement of environmental laws, regulations, and policies.
Join us for a series of conversations surrounding environmental justice issues, hosted by the Law Center Staff and community members.
