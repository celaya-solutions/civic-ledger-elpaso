Case Work: Water
LANL Radioactive Liquid Waste Treatment Facility Discharge Permit
LOCATION: Los Alamos
INITIATION DATE: June 2004
CLIENTS/PARTNERS: Communities for Clean Water
OPPOSING PARTIES: Los Alamos National Laboratory, New Mexico Environment Department, Department of Energy
STATUS: Closed
ATTORNEYS:
SUMMARY:
The Law Center is representing Communities for Clean Water in state proceedings concerning Discharge Permit 1132 for Los Alamos National Laboratory’s Radioactive Liquid Waste Treatment Facility. We are challenging the regulation of the Facility under a Clean Water Act permit instead of a more relevant – and stringent – Resource Conservation and Recovery Act (RCRA) permit
