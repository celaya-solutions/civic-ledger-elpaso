The Breakdown - The Great Trade Debate
The Breakdown: The Truth About Tariffs
What exactly are tariffs and how do they affect your wallet, your job, and international relations? In this episode, host Brad Montgomery sits down with Professor Carlos Aguilar, Economics expert at EPCC, to unpack the often-misunderstood world of tariffs. From trade wars to local impacts on the El Paso economy, they explore the real-world consequences of protectionist policies, common misconceptions about trade deficits, and why tariffs don’t always work the way we expect.
Whether you’re curious about global economics or how it affects your hometown, this episode helps you understand the hidden forces shaping our economy.
