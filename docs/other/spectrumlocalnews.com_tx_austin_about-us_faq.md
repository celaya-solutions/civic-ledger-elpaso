How do I log in to watch videos?
Locate the words “Log In” at the top right corner of the screen (desktop) or in the drop down menu (mobile). Alternatively, you can log in by following the prompts upon viewing a video. Your username and password are the same credentials assigned to you upon subscribing to Spectrum services.
