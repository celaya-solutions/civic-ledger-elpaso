YOUR LOCAL GUIDE TO THINGS TO DO IN AND AROUND AUSTIN
There are so many things to do in and around Austin. Here's your one-stop guide to what's happening, from food to attractions to concerts and events...it's all here. Get the latest updates on Events in the area, what's new in the Austin Food scene, and news from area Attractions.
Austin Events
Austin is well-known for events like SXSW and ACL, but there's so much more to do in the Capital City.
While tourists may focus on Sixth Street, we Austinites know some of the best events are those that only we locals know about. Every week, there are things to do, from pop-up flea markets, to indie showcases, to movies in the park.
Austin Food
Let's face it, if there's one thing we know how to do in Austin, it's eat. It's a food lover's playground. Of course there are the staples that everyone know about, from Eddie V's to Kerby Lane, but we locals know the Austin food scene boasts hundreds of unique eateries.
There are even raging debates about the best BBQ in the city, where you can get the best Tex-Mex, and which food trucks are truly to die for.
Austin Attractions
There are so many places to explore in and around Austin from tours of the spectacular Capitol building, to sprawling parks, to water parks like Schlitterbahn.
But what if you want to avoid the tourist spots and still expore the city? There are still plenty of places to go that cater to locals. Take, for example, this list of some of the city's most prominent murals. Follow the map and see where it leads!
