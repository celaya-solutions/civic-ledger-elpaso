The 15 biggest pop culture moments of the year!

by Elizabeth Stanton

Wed, December 31, 2025 at 7:00 AM
16VIEW ALL PHOTOS
FILE - Travis Kelce, left, and Taylor Swift pose after the AFC Championship NFL football game against the Buffalo Bills, Jan. 26, 2025, in Kansas City, Mo. (AP Photo/Ashley Landis, File)

Before we kick off the new year, we’ve got a look back at the best, weirdest, saddest, and most memorable pop culture moments from 2025.

Katy Perry, Gayle King, and Lauren Bezos Visit Space

This image provided by Blue Origin shows, first row, seated, from left: Lauren Sanchez and Kerianne Flynn and standing in back from left: Amanda Nguyen, Katy Perry, Gayle King and Aisha Bowe in West Texas. (Blue Origin via AP)

Katy Perry, Gayle King, and Lauren Sanchez Bezos were launched into space in April, part of the first all-female flight crew for Blue Origin. Activist Amanda Nguyen, NASA rocket scientist Aisha Bowe and film producer Kerianne Flynn completed the six-person crew, which spent about 10 minutes 60 miles above the earth in zero gravity.

Sean “Diddy” Combs Goes to Jail

FILE - Sean Combs arrives at the Pre-Grammy Gala And Salute To Industry Icons at the Beverly Hilton Hotel on Saturday, Jan. 25, 2020, in Beverly Hills, Calif. (Photo by Mark Von Holden/Invision/AP, File)

Over a year and a half after the FBI first raided his home in March 2024, Sean “Diddy” Combs was sentenced to four years in prison on October 3, 2025, after being found guilty on two counts of transportation to engage in prostitution in July. The sentence came after a lengthy trial and amid continued speculation about possible other crimes, though he was only convicted on the two charges.

Coldplay Kiss Cam Scandal

RIO DE JANEIRO, BRAZIL - SEPTEMBER 10: Chris Martin of the band Coldplay performs at the Mundo Stage during the Rock in Rio Festival at Cidade do Rock on September 10, 2022 in Rio de Janeiro, Brazil. The famous festival Rock in Rio returns after two years of cancellation due to COVID-19 pandemic. (Photo by Buda Mendes/Getty Images)

It started with a seemingly innocent cuddle that was caught on the Jumbotron during a Coldplay concert in July until the couple, Andy Byron and Kristin Cabot, realized they were on camera. They quickly separated and tried to hide from view, prompting Coldplay lead singer Chris Martin to jokingly ask if they were having an affair. Media chaos ensued, and by the time the dust settled, both Byron and Cabot were out at their jobs, though they’ve denied any actual affair, with Cabot recently calling it a “bad decision.”

Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce Get Engaged

FILE - Travis Kelce, left, and Taylor Swift pose after the AFC Championship NFL football game against the Buffalo Bills, Jan. 26, 2025, in Kansas City, Mo. (AP Photo/Ashley Landis, File)

It feels “So High School,” but we can’t help but be a little giddy that Taylor Swift, the mastermind behind many a breakup anthem, and Travis Kelce, the goofy, good-hearted Kansas City Chiefs tight end are going to walk down the aisle! As Taylor wrote in the caption for their stunning engagement pics, “Your English teacher and your gym teacher are getting married.”

Kendrick Lamar/Drake Beef

FILE - Kendrick Lamar performs during halftime of the NFL Super Bowl 59 football game between the Kansas City Chiefs and the Philadelphia Eagles, Feb. 9, 2025, in New Orleans. (AP Photo/Matt Slocum, file)

It’s one thing to go back and forth with a rival rapper in song; it’s quite another to bring that beef to the Super Bowl, one of the biggest stages in the world. But Kendrick Lamar did just that in February, effectively putting the final nail in the coffin of their longstanding feud. When you get millions of people singing along to lyrics with some serious allegations, where do you go from there?

Sydney Sweeney Jeans Ad

PHOTO CREDIT: American Eagle

Who knew a simple advertisement about a pair of jeans could cause such a stir? Well, Brooke Shields probably, but Sydney Sweeney learned that lesson this year with her American Eagle jeans ad. The wordplay on Sweeney having “good genes/jeans” prompted polarized responses that came from everyone, including the White House. Sweeney tried to stay quiet amid the storm, but later opened up that it didn’t quite work, telling People, "In the past my stance has been to never respond to negative or positive press but recently I have come to realize that my silence regarding this issue has only widened the divide, not closed it.”

KPop Demon Hunters Takes Over

(L-R) Audrey Nuna, EJAE and Rei Ami attend the KPop Demon Hunters Special Screening at Netflix Tudum Theater on June 16, 2025 in Los Angeles, California. (Credit: Charley Gallay/Getty Images for Netflix)

If you don’t know the words to “Golden” from “KPop Demon Hunters” by now, we can’t help you. The Netflix animated feature was THE movie of the summer (and possibly the year), with infectious ear worm songs, a fun visual style, and a charming story that blended supernatural, action, comedy, and musical elements perfectly. The soundtrack earned five Grammy nominations, and the film earned two Golden Globes nominations. Place your bets now for some kind of Oscar nomination in the new year.

The Year of the Mormon Wives

THE SECRET LIVES OF MORMON WIVES - Season 2 - Key Art. (Disney)

The Mormon Wives have transcended their TikTok origins this year with a full pop culture take over. The third season of the reality show was a hit (and introduced us to new drama somehow related to Fruity Pebbles cereal), while several stars moved on to mainstream projects. Whitney Leavitt and Jen Affleck both competed on “Dancing with the Stars,” with Whitney landing a role in “Chicago” on Broadway and her first movie gig as well after her elimination, while their co-star Taylor Frankie Paul was named the next “Bachelorette,” a major shakeup for the dating reality series.

Cowboy Carter

LOS ANGELES, CALIFORNIA - FEBRUARY 02: (FOR EDITORIAL USE ONLY) Beyonce accepts the Album of the Year for “Cowboy Carter” onstage during the 67th GRAMMY Awards at Crypto.com Arena on February 02, 2025 in Los Angeles, California. (Photo by Amy Sussman/Getty Images)

Going country paid off big time for Beyoncé this year. Her 2024 album, “Cowboy Carter,” which featured the hit single “Texas Hold ‘Em,” took home a Grammy for Best Country Album and Album of the Year at this year’s Grammy ceremony, marking some major historical milestones. Beyonce is the first Black artist in 50 years to win in the country categories and the first Black woman EVER to win Best Country Album, as well as the first Black woman in 25 years to win Album of the Year, not to mention her first time EVER winning that highly coveted award. She took the album on the road with a stunning tour that became the highest-grossing country tour of all time. What genre will Queen Bey conquer in 2026?

Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban's Divorce

HOLLYWOOD, CALIFORNIA - APRIL 27: (L-R) Keith Urban and honoree Nicole Kidman attend the 49th Annual AFI Life Achievement Award Honoring Nicole Kidman at Dolby Theatre on April 27, 2024 in Hollywood, California. (Photo by Emma McIntyre/Getty Images for Warner Bros. Discovery)

This is one we truly never saw coming. We thought Nicole Kidman and Keith Urban were in it for the long haul. Kidman was the one who filed for divorce in September, after 19 years together, and sources at the time said they were already living separately since the beginning of the summer. Reports from TMZ claimed Urban had already moved on with another woman, but neither party has spoken publicly about their breakup.

'Dancing with the Stars' Has Its Best Season in Years

DANCING WITH THE STARS - “Finale”- Capping off a record-breaking season, the finale episode sees the five remaining couples competing for a chance at winning the coveted Len Goodman Mirrorball Trophy. TUESDAY, NOV. 25 (8:00-11:00 p.m. EST) on ABC. (Disney/Eric McCandless) DANIELLA KARAGACH, HILARIA BALDWIN, WHITNEY LEAVITT, ALAN BERSTEN, JAN RAVNIK, ROBERT IRWIN, WITNEY CARSON

Something about Season 34 of “Dancing with the Stars” hit different. Maybe it’s that the season coincided with the show’s 20th birthday and production brought out all the stops with theme nights dedicated to “Wicked,” The Rock and Roll Hall of Fame, and Prince. Maybe it was the line up, with “Mormon Wives” stars Whitney Leavitt and Jen Affleck, the incredible charming Robert Irwin, and the breakout social media star Andy Richter. Maybe it was the full-on embrace of TikTok by the pros and celebs alike that connected with audiences. It was probably all of those things, because the finale, which saw Irwin take home the Mirrorball trophy, earned the show’s highest ratings since 2016.

Hollywood Tragedies

WASHINGTON, DC - APRIL 28: Rob Reiber and Michele Singer Reiner attends the 2018 White House Correspondents' Dinner at Washington Hilton on April 28, 2018 in Washington, DC. (Photo by Tasos Katopodis/Getty Images)

2025 was bookended by the losses of two Hollywood icons through shocking means. Gene Hackman and his wife, Betsy Arakawa, were both found dead in their home in February. It was later determined that Arakawa had contracted hantavirus and died, while Hackman struggled to survive on his own due to advanced Alzheimer’s and other health conditions, dying less than a week later.

FILE - Actor Gene Hackman arrives with his wife, Betsy Arakawa, for the 60th Annual Golden Globe Awards in Beverly Hills, Calif., Jan. 19, 2003. (AP Photo/Mark J. Terrill, File)

And in December, Rob Reiner and his wife Michele were found dead in their home by homicide, allegedly perpetrated by their son, Nick Reiner. It was a sad start and end to the year for Hollywood, coupled with other losses throughout the year (see our full gallery of stars we lost here).

Jelly Roll’s Makeover

NASHVILLE, TENNESSEE - NOVEMBER 23: Artist Jelly Roll looks on from the tunnel prior to the game between the Seattle Seahawks and the Tennessee Titans at Nissan Stadium on November 23, 2025 in Nashville, Tennessee. (Photo by Johnnie Izquierdo/Getty Images)

Jelly Roll still has distinctive face tattoos, but the singer has revamped his look over the past year, ditching the beard and dropping an incredible 200 pounds. Speaking on “The Joe Rogan Experience,” the now 41-year-old revealed he decided to make healthy changes to lose weight on his 39th birthday. "I thought about it around my birthday ‘cause I knew my next one was 40. You know what, I don't think I've ever met a 500-pound 40-year-old,” he said, adding he “could feel myself dying.” Congratulations Jelly Roll on your hard work, and cheers to a healthy new year!

The 'Wicked' Tour Part 2

Cynthia Erivo, left, and Ariana Grande attend the premiere of "Wicked: For Good" at Lincoln Center, Monday, Nov. 17, 2025, in New York. (Photo by Evan Agostini/Invision/AP)

It was one short year between “Wicked: Part One” and “Wicked: For Good,” and we got another fashionable, funny, and memorable press tour leading up to the film’s release. There was Michelle Yeoh pointing out the MM for Madame Morrible upside down meant Wicked Witch, Cynthia Erivo protecting Ariana Grande from a red carpet crasher, and Jonathan Bailey being named Sexiest Man Alive, to name a few. What are we going to do next year with nothing “Wicked” on the horizon?!

'Stranger Things' Finale

STRANGER THINGS: SEASON 5. Noah Schnapp as Will Byers in Stranger Things: Season 5. Cr. COURTESY OF NETFLIX © 2025

We won’t post any spoilers here juuuust in case you haven’t caught up with the show before its finale tonight on New Year’s Eve, but the end of “Stranger Things” is truly the end of an era. It’s been five seasons and almost 10 years since the 80s throwback series debuted, giving us memorable moments and monsters, not to mention memes, Halloween costumes, and a renewed understanding of Dungeons and Dragons.
